Dan C. Middlebrooks, Executive Vice President – Northern Field Gathering and Processing, 
Dan C. Middlebrooks has served as Executive Vice President —,  Northern Field Gathering and Processing since November 2015. Mr. Middlebrooks previously served Targa as Senior Vice President Field Gathering and Processing between June 2014 and November 2015 and as Vice President of Field Gathering and Processing from April 2010 to June 2014. Mr. Middlebrooks joined Targa in 2004 and has held several other supply and processing positions within Targa from 2004 to 2010. Prior to joining Targa, Mr. Middlebrooks was the founder and President of a private midstream company from 1986 until its sale in 2000. From 1986 to 2004, Mr. Middlebrooks was also part owner of a private exploration company. From 1981 to 1986, Mr. Middlebrooks worked for Producer's Gas Company a subsidiary of Lear Petroleum Corporation in the gas supply and gas marketing departments. From 1980 to 1981, Mr. Middlebrooks worked for Delhi Gas Pipeline Company a subsidiary of Texas Oil and Gas Corporation.
Clark White, Executive Vice President – Engineering and Operations, 
Clark White has served as Executive Vice President —,  Engineering and Operations since November 2015. Mr. White previously served as Senior Vice President – Field G&P between June 2014 and November 2015. He joined Targa in 2005 through Targa’s acquisition of Dynegy Midstream Services LP, serving as a Vice President - Field G&P for the Permian Basin and North Texas until May 2014. He previously served as Vice President - Field G&P at Dynegy Midstream Services LP from 1998 to 2005. Mr. White held engineering and operations positions from 1990 to 1998 with Natural Gas Clearinghouse, which became Dynegy in 1997. He held Gas Supply and Business Development positions from 1997 to 1990 with Tejas Gas Corp. Prior to 1990, he held various engineering and operations positions with Gulf Energy Development and Perry Gas Companies.
