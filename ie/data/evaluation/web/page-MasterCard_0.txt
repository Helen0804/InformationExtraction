Andrea Cotroneo
As Canada Region Counsel, Andrea Cotroneo is responsible for leading the legal function for MasterCard Canada.
Ms. Cotroneo brings vast in-house counsel and law firm experience to MasterCard, including extensive knowledge in the areas of corporate, commercial, consumer, marketing, privacy and e-commerce law. Prior to joining MasterCard, she worked as legal counsel for both Dell Canada and Moneris Solutions. Ms. Cotroneo began her career in private practice with Aird & Berlis LLP, where she practiced corporate and securities law.
Ms. Cotroneo holds an Honours Bachelor of Arts degree in Political Science and Languages from Laurentian University and a Bachelor of Laws degree from the University of Ottawa.
